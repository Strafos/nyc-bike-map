🚲 NYC + Philly Car-Free Bike Map

Every notable greenway & rail trail within the NYC day-trip orbit, plus a Philly rail extension by Amtrak/NJT/SEPTA, across NY, NJ, CT, Long Island & southeast PA — nearly all majority car-free dedicated path, plus a couple of legendary on-road classics (amber % badge). On the map, solid lines are dedicated car-free path and dashed colored lines are on-road sections — the car-free % on each card is measured from OpenStreetMap data, with Philly named-trail corridors pinned as verified trail overlays where OSM way tags under-report the path.
