🚲 NYC Car-Free Bike Map

Every notable greenway & rail trail within a 2-hour journey of Midtown Manhattan by bike, train or ferry, in all directions across NY, NJ, CT & Long Island — nearly all majority car-free dedicated path, plus a couple of legendary on-road classics (amber % badge). On the map, solid lines are dedicated car-free path and dashed colored lines are on-road sections — the car-free % on each card is measured from OpenStreetMap data.

Route lines follow real paths (OpenStreetMap data via BRouter). Dashed gray lines are train, subway or ferry transfers. Distances are one-way unless marked as loops. Since 2023 no bike permit is needed on Metro-North or the LIRR; bikes ride the subway and Staten Island Ferry anytime, NYC Ferry for $1, Amtrak with a $20 reservation. Avoid rush-hour trains.
