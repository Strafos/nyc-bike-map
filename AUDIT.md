# Coverage Audit — every transit line & county within 2 hours of Midtown Manhattan

Method: every transit line/branch serving the 2-hour radius was checked for majority-car-free
trails near its stations, cross-referenced against Rails-to-Trails Conservancy / TrailLink,
Wikipedia's rail-trail lists, county parks departments, trail association sites, and
transit-to-trails guides (MTA Away, Discover Long Island). Status: ✅ drawn route ·
📝 honorable mention (too short/fragmentary) · 🚫 nothing qualifying · 🔜 under construction.
